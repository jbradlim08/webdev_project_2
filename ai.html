Known for its AI-powered virtual assistant Alexa, Amazon also uses AI extensively in its e-commerce platform for product recommendations, logistics optimization, and customer service.

Google

Google is a leader in AI research and development, with products like Google Search, Google Assistant, Google Photos, and YouTube leveraging AI for various tasks such as natural language processing, image recognition, and content recommendation.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an AI-powered conversational agent developed by OpenAI. It's based on the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) architecture, specifically trained to understand and generate human-like text in response to user input. ChatGPT can engage in a wide range of conversations, answer questions, provide information, and even generate creative content like stories or poems.
